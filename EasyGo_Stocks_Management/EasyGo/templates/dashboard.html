Objective

In the business world there must be an objective to further develop the business. The main objective of the EasyGo Stock Management system is to provide satisfactory service to students at KPMB and prevent unwanted things from happening such as selling damaged or expired items to students. In addition, by selling cheaper items to students to provide student-friendly prices.

Importance of stock management systems

In the stock management system there are many advantages, one of which is that the goods will be sold more regularly and consistently. In addition, if there are unwanted things such as damaged items or lost items with this system it makes it easier for the staff to manage the matter.In many good there must be bad as well. Purpose of this system

To maintain an appropriate stock level of all items, avoiding shortages and oversupply.

Staff can maintain sufficient stock for items. If the item is decreasing, the staff can provide sufficient stock supply for the item. So the problem of running out of stock can be eliminated.

It is easy for the staff to check whether the stock of the item needs to be ordered or issued. If the stock of the item has decreased, the staff will order new stock, if the item is too much, the staff will issue the item.
